# Takeaways and lessons learned

1. A 2-syllable word has the emphasis on the second syllable
2. In a 3-syllable word, the emphasis is like 2-1-3, with a higher number representing more emphasis
3. Always use Pinyin in the earlier stages to understand how a word is pronounced
4. The audio in the tool I am creating is pretty accurate
5. For "not have", it is not 不有, but 没有 (méiyǒu)
6. Words learned:
