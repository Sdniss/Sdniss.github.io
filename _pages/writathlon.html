Write Scheduling

Inspired by Joe Friel's The Triathlete's Training Bible.

Write Scheduling is inspired by the concept of "periodisation" to prepare for a triathlon. In his book "The Triathlete's Training Bible", expert coach Joe Friel explains how preparing for a big sports event is best done counting backwards from race date.

Preparation time is subdivided in blocks that each have a unique goal, such as building aerobic endurance or muscular endurance. When the blocks are defined, you can further zoom in to plan a specific week with actionable to do's.

I see many similarities with writing. Writing requires planning, and also has different phases. Researching, writing, editing, etc. In the Write Scheduling, I experiment with a new way of scheduling your writing. The planning coarsely follows a periodisation plan, which is then personalised using an LLM and your unique writing style, for which you can choose from the writing style of different famous writers.
