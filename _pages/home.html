A bit of background

This brain age model was trained on data from three open source datasets, distributed across three centers across Europe. For more information, please refer to Denissen et al. 2026. I was 27 years old at the time my brain MRI was acquired, so my brain is looking too old! I hope that my triathlon training, which I started afterwards, is paying off now! Nonetheless, brain age models, especially those relying on deep neural networks such as this one, are known to have poor generalisability, meaning that they do not work very well on data from other centers, scanners, ...

So take the prediction with a pinch of salt, but I thought it's cool to show that AI research can be transformed into an interface that users can try!
