My academic journey

Where I am

Over the years, I have built an interdisciplinary profile as physio with hands-on AI experience. I am now leading the iAIMS community connecting researchers worldwide to leverage this interdisciplinary view towards real clinical impact with AI.

Where I'm going

As a junior post-doctoral fellow of the FWO, I combine Federated Learning, Brain Age and Explainable AI to investigate the relationship between brain damage and cognitive impairment in MS. I dream to ultimately find targets for exercise to slow down accelerated cognitive deterioration and brain aging in MS.
